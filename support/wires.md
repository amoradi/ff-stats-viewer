[d] [k] [qb] [rb] [te] [wr] - **(divs, multiple selection capable)**

Choose Scoring Method - **(radio)**

- standard
- ppr
- half ppr

Choose Stats Mode - **(radio)**

- sum (stats from single year totals)

[Choose Year] - **(select)**
